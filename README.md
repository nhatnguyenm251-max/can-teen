# Can-teen

## Title
**Can-teen: Tin dung bua an cho sinh vien tren Stellar**

## Description
Can-teen la du an smart contract giup nha truong va can-tin quan ly "tin dung bua an" cho sinh vien tren Stellar. Thay vi theo doi thu cong hoac su dung cac he thong dong, moi giao dich nap credit, tieu credit va chuyen credit deu duoc ghi nhan minh bach tren blockchain.

Muc dich cua du an:
- So hoa quy trinh ho tro bua an cho sinh vien.
- Giup can-tin giam sai sot khi doi soat.
- Tang tinh minh bach trong viec cap phat va su dung credit.

Tai sao lam idea nay:
- Nhu cau thuc te trong moi truong truong hoc la rat ro rang.
- Soroban phu hop de xay logic don gian, de mo rong va de audit.
- Co the phat trien thanh he thong phuc loi hoc duong quy mo lon.

## Tinh nang
- Khoi tao contract va metadata token noi bo (`initialize`).
- Admin nap them tin dung cho sinh vien (`top_up`).
- Sinh vien su dung tin dung de thanh toan bua an (`spend`).
- Chuyen credit giua cac tai khoan sinh vien (`transfer`).
- Doi admin quan ly he thong (`set_admin`).
- Truy van so du tung sinh vien va tong cung credit (`balance`, `total_supply`).
- Phat event cho cac hanh dong quan trong de theo doi lich su giao dich.
